Audio Escalation Matrix

We don't just shout. Our agents use intelligent judgment. The tone of voice changes based on the behavior of the individual.

Level 1: Advisory

Non-threatening behavior (e.g., loitering teens)

"Good evening. This is a private area. Please be advised that the facility is closed. Thank you for exiting."

Level 2: Warning

Suspicious activity (e.g., looking into cars)

"Security Alert. You in the blue jacket. Step away from the vehicle. Your actions are being recorded and monitored."

Level 3: Critical

Criminal intent (e.g., breaking glass, fence climbing)

"STOP! THIS IS SECURITY. POLICE HAVE BEEN DISPATCHED. LEAVE THE PREMISES IMMEDIATELY OR YOU WILL BE ARRESTED."
