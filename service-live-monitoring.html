LIVE VIDEO MONITORING

Turn your passive CCTV cameras into a proactive crime-fighting tool. Our intelligent analytics and human operators watch your property in real-time, intervening the moment a threat is detected.

How It Works

Traditional CCTV is reactive—you only look at the footage after a theft has occurred. Live Video Monitoring is proactive. We connect your cameras to our Mohali Command Center using secure internet protocols.

1

Intelligent Detection

Advanced analytics scan video feeds 24/7 for specific threats: people, vehicles, or weapons. Movements like swaying trees, rain, or stray animals are automatically filtered out.

2

Human Verification

When the system flags a threat, the live video feed pops up on a priority screen in our Command Center. A trained operator verifies within seconds if it is a real crime in progress.

3

Immediate Action

We activate on-site sirens, issue stern audio warnings via loudspeakers ("Leave the premises immediately!"), and dispatch local police with a verified crime report.
