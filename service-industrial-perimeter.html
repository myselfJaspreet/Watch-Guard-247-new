Breach Protocol: What Happens?

00:00 - Breach Detected

Subject crosses virtual tripwire. Thermal camera identifies heat signature. System filters out wildlife.

00:03 - Command Center Alert

Live feed opens on priority screen in Mohali. Operator assesses threat level.

00:10 - Active Deterrence

Operator activates on-site strobe lights and 120dB siren. Issues voice command: "Security Alert. Leave immediately."

00:30 - Dispatch

If intruder persists, local police and client emergency contacts are notified with description and location.
