Our Technology

HOW THE "VIRTUAL EYE" WORKS

A seamless integration of hardware, software, and human intelligence.

1. AI Detection

The camera doesn't just record; it "sees." It distinguishes between a stray dog, a swaying tree, and a human climbing a fence. False alarms are filtered at the edge.

2. Encrypted Transmission

Upon detecting a threat, the footage is compressed and sent via secure, redundant internet lines (with cellular backup) to our Mohali Command Center.

3. Rapid Response

Our agents receive the alert in < 3 seconds. They use two-way audio to warn the intruder and dispatch local police with verified crime reports.

THE AMRITSAR COMMAND CENTER

Local Expertise

Redundancy
